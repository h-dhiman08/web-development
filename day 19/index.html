Reflections of a Quiet Mind

Beneath the stars, in silence deep,
The poet dreams, yet cannot sleep.
With every line, a world is born,
Of hearts that heal, of souls that mourn.

Whispers of the Wind

The wind it speaks in gentle rhyme,
Of passing years and fleeting time.
It tells of love that once did glow,
And vanished like the evening snow.
